About Citizen AI

Citizen AI is a groundbreaking project focused on revolutionizing the interaction between government and citizens. By harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, our mission is to create a more transparent, efficient, and responsive governance system, fostering greater civic engagement and trust.

In Simple Terms

Citizen AI is your digital bridge to the government. It’s a smart assistant that helps you:

  • Ask Questions: Get quick, reliable answers to your queries about government services and policies.
  • Report Issues: Easily report problems in your community, like a broken street light or a garbage collection issue.
  • Share Feedback: Provide feedback on public services, which helps us understand what’s working and what isn’t.

Our Core Platform

Key Features

Our platform is built on cutting-edge technology, including **IBM Granite Models**, to deliver powerful and accurate results.

  • Chat Assistant: An AI-powered chatbot that provides instant, intelligent responses to citizen inquiries.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Automatically analyzes feedback to gauge public sentiment (positive, neutral, or negative) on various government services.
  • Concern Reporting: A streamlined process for citizens to report issues and track their resolution.

Why It's Useful

Citizen AI offers tangible benefits for both citizens and government bodies:

  • Efficiency & Transparency: Get answers and report issues without the hassle of navigating complex bureaucratic systems.
  • Enhanced Engagement: Provides a direct, easy-to-use channel for civic participation, making citizens feel heard.
  • Improved Decision Making: Government officials can use real-time sentiment data and issue reports to make informed decisions and allocate resources effectively.
